# Randy \- a personal RAND Corp

## A personal advisory committee and think tank for strategic planning

# Main idea

User consult a group of experts and smart people, like in RAND Corp, for strategic questions. This user can be anyone, instead of presidents and top officials. Questions can be bigger than daily work and life, for example

* Medium to long term career planning  
* Medium to long term personal health planning and optimization  
* Long term family and relationship building  
* Explore innovative ideas for startups and new enterprise

Three experts

* Function: deep research of strategic questions, analysis, evaluate, synthesize, hypothesis, scenario simulation, review and iterate. Provide deep insight and suggestions. Act as trusted advisors, with objective and independent thinking.  
* Candidates:  
  * OpenAI GPT-5.2 Pro  
  * Claude Opus 4.5  
  * DeepSeek v3.2 Speciale

One facilitator/secretary

* Function: interact with human, plan, prioritize, coordinate, summarize, compile  
* Candidate:  
  * Google Gemini 3 Pro (with Nano Banana)

# Potential challenges

1. Compatibility and uniformity across different LLMs  
   1. different input/output format  
   2. how to communicate among the agents in multi-modal format  
2. How to facilitate tool calling and tool use for external data and API across different LLMs?  
3. What is the best UI/UX that this personal advisory committee interact with human user?  
   1. Text, image, voice, video?  
   2. Round table meeting?  
4. How autonomous this setup should be?  
   1. One-time execution  
   2. Multi-stage agentic flow with self reflection, self evaluation and action?
